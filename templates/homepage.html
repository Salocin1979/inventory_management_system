Our Concept

Nestled in the heart of Ruisseau Créole in the coastal region of Rivière Noire, Le Kiosk is a contemporary tropical eatery celebrating the vibrant flavors of Mauritius. Our concept blends casual sophistication with a strong commitment to fresh, local, and authentic cuisine.

We specialize in fresh, seasonal dishes inspired by Mauritian Creole cuisine, French-inspired bistro fare, and light Mediterranean influences. Our menu highlights locally sourced seafood and meats, garden-fresh vegetables, and house-made sauces and dressings. We proudly offer vegetarian and gluten-free options, with signature dishes rotating to celebrate the best of each season.

The vibe at Le Kiosk is tropical & contemporary, casual yet refined – ideal for long lunches, sunset apéros, or relaxed dinners. Surrounded by palm trees and open air, our space features natural wood, woven textures, and soft lighting, all complemented by chilled, curated music to create a laid-back island-luxe feel.
